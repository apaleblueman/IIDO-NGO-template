Make a Difference, One Wag at a Time

Welcome to the volunteer page of the International Indie Dogs Organisation (IIDO)! Our mission to save the world, one wag at a time, is driven by the dedication and passion of our incredible volunteers. If you love dogs and want to make a meaningful impact, we invite you to join our team and help us give indie dogs the love and care they deserve.

Why Volunteer with IIDO?

Volunteering with IIDO is a rewarding experience that allows you to:

  • Save Lives:Your efforts directly contribute to the rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming of indie dogs.
  • Learn and Grow: Gain valuable skills and knowledge in animal care, rescue operations, and nonprofit work.
  • Build Community:Connect with fellow dog lovers and become part of a supportive and passionate community.
  • Make a Difference: Every hour you volunteer helps improve the lives of dogs in need and promotes the adoption of indie dogs.

Volunteer Opportunities

We offer a variety of volunteer roles to match your interests, skills, and availability:

  • Dog Care: Help with feeding, grooming, and exercising our dogs. Provide them with the love and attention they need to thrive.
  • Foster Homes: Open your home to a dog in need. Fostering provides a temporary, loving environment until a forever home is found.
  • Adoption Events: Assist at adoption events by setting up, greeting potential adopters, and helping showcase our dogs.
  • Fundraising and Outreach: Help plan and execute fundraising events, spread awareness about our mission, and engage with the community.
  • Transport: Assist with transporting dogs to and from veterinary appointments, foster homes, and adoption events.
  • Administrative Support: Provide behind-the-scenes support with tasks such as data entry, communications, and organizing supplies.

How to Get Started

Becoming a volunteer with IIDO is easy! Follow these simple steps to join our team:

  • Fill Out the Application: Complete our online volunteer application form here. Provide us with some basic information about yourself and your interests.
  • Attend Orientation: Join us for a volunteer orientation session to learn more about our mission, procedures, and how you can help.
  • Start Volunteering: Once you've completed orientation, you’ll be ready to start making a difference! We’ll work with you to find the volunteer role that best fits your skills and interests.
