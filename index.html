Our Mission

At IIDO, our mission is to save the world, one wag at a time. We are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming indie dogs, providing them with the love, care, and second chances they deserve.

Through advocacy, education, and community engagement, we strive to break stereotypes, promote adoption, and create a world where every dog has a safe and loving home. Together, we can make tails wag and hearts smile, one paw print at a time
